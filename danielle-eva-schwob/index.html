SHOWCASING THE DUALITY OF A COMPOSER AND ALTERNATIVE ARTIST.

Danielle Eva Schwob is one of those rare talents that can create an orchestral arrangement as effortlessly as she can write alternative pop songs. But with this talent comes the challenge of not being pigeon-holed into either camp.

A FOCUS ON DUALITY

We worked with Danielle to rethink her digital presence and build a compelling narrative showing off the beauty in her duality while allowing both sides of her story to breathe and yet stay tethered to each other through common threads. The result is a stunning site allowing viewers to seamlessly move between her worlds while immersing themselves in the wonder of her work.
