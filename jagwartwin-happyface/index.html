Even while the world is burning around us, we put on a “happy face”, hold a smile and continue going on with our “normal lives”. With this idea as the backbone to Jagwar Twin’s new single, we concepted and executed a release strategy to bring fans not only into the world of Jagwar Twin, but also into a digital experience where they could immerse themselves into an upside down world, inquiring what it really means to be Happy in society.

2.0 Driving Anticipation

LIGHTING A FANBASE ON FIRE

Months into the dormancy of COVID, the Jagwar Twin fan base had been enjoying weekly guided meditations together, but had no inkling that new music was coming down the pipeline. To create a buzz we dropped cryptic social posts simply linking to Deathofmygeneration.fun with no explanation.

This cryptic landing page featured a bunch of seemingly disconnected copy rambling about the world gone mad and happiness while prominently featuring a countdown and a phone number asking fans a simple question “Are you content?”

Fans could sign up to receive launch drips via SMS and if they called the phone number, they would be met with a manifesto asking them to leave a message answering the question “Are you content or content?”

4.0 SITE LAUNCH

The Smile-Triggered Unlock

At the end of the countdown, we unveiled the song title and branding through a series of manifesto-like social posts that drilled home the meaning of the song while leaving the fans with a a final thought …  “There is a test involved, like there is in every moment, but my hope is that it will make you think about the world you want to create and help you to see yourself more clearly.”

The site opened with a bomb going off behind a happy face following the user’s mouse cursor throughout the screen. After giving access to the camera, fans are asked to look into the camera and put on a happy face. Through facial recognition we are able to check the fans emotion and, once they are happy, start a countdown.

Fans who held their smile were treated to hearing the song before it was available on DSPs but if the user started to lose the smile we would flash “SMILE MORE” at them. Lose the smile altogether and the song came to a screeching halt.

deathofmygeneration.fun

At the end of the experience, fans were presented with an animated GIF of their experience which dynamically showed them how long they lasted. This content was shareable to all social media platforms, amplifying visibility for the experience.

5.0 GATED MERCH CAPSULE

BUY!BUY!BUY!

"Hollywood on your timeline telling you what to wear and what to like and how to be!"

We wanted fans to hear these lyrics and ask themselves if they are the user or the product, so we created a limited edition merch capsule, backed by a custom e-commerce store, that could only be unlocked by sitting through the whole song without losing a smile.
